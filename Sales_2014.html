df['YearMonth'] = df['Order Date'].dt.to_period('M')

for i, row in monthly_sales.iterrows():

plt.title('Monthly Total Sales')
plt.xlabel('Month')

plt.xticks(rotation=45)

Recommendations

Summary: From the analysis of sales and shipping data, several key findings emerged. Richard outperformed others in overall sales performance when considering both total sales and order count, while Bob had the highest number of orders. Monthly sales showed clear seasonal fluctuations, with notable drops in May and June. The correlation study revealed that higher discounts negatively impacted sales, indicating that excessive discounting might harm revenue. Additionally, shipping costs were higher for low-priority orders and large product containers. Recommendation: To reduce shipping costs, it is advisable to use slower, more economical shipping methods for low-priority orders and optimize packaging to reduce container sizes. Furthermore, discount strategies should be adjusted by lowering discount rates during months with declining sales to maintain profitability. Implementing these measures will enhance cost efficiency and stabilize revenue growth
